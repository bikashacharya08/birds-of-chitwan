Rare Sighting Record Justice for Rojan Subedi

Javan Pond Heron Ardeola speciosa (जाभा बुङ्गे बकुल्ला)

In Sauraha village, local guide Rojan Subedi and local observers documented a sighting of the Javan Pond Heron. Despite strong verification from the local guiding community who observed the bird, it was not officially recognized by the national rarities authority. This field guide dedicates this space to Rojan's discovery, standing in solidarity with local expertise.
